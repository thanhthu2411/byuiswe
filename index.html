WELCOME TO SWE AT BYU-IDAHO

Empowering, inspiring, and supporting women in engineering and technology. At SWE BYU-Idaho, we are a community dedicated to helping women succeed in STEM through mentorship, professional development, and networking opportunities. Whether you’re a student exploring engineering, a seasoned professional, or an ally, we invite you to join us in building a future of innovation and inclusivity.
